Who built this

I'm Nyonka Vincent Tafeli — a warehouse operations specialist with a BSc in Pure Mathematics and C1 (Advanced) English. That combination is the point: I don't just run a warehouse floor, I think about it the way a mathematician thinks about a system — as a set of inputs, constraints, and measurable outputs. DrinkTrack Pro is what happens when that lens gets pointed at a real operational problem instead of a textbook one.

01

The problem

EKANE & SONS moves high volumes of drinks stock daily — purchases, sales, crate deposits, supplier credit, customer debt — all of it originally tracked by hand. That works until it doesn't: stock counts drift, debts get forgotten, and nobody can answer "what do we actually have right now?" without a physical recount.

I'd studied Pure Mathematics before moving into warehouse operations, and I kept seeing this as a data problem as much as an operations one. So I built the tool I actually needed on the floor.
